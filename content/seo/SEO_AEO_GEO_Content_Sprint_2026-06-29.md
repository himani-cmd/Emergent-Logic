# Emergent Logic SEO / AEO / GEO Content Sprint

Prepared: 2026-06-29  
Owner: SEO / AEO / GEO Content Agent  
Scope: 30-day content plan for revenue-focused organic growth across Canada and the US.

## Strategic Goal

Build topical authority around practical CRM revenue operations problems: CRM cleanup, HubSpot consulting, Salesforce consulting, CRM implementation, Salesforce admin support, marketing automation, lead capture systems, AI automation, CRM data cleanup, and website-leads-to-CRM workflows.

This sprint should help Emergent Logic appear in:

- Google search results for high-intent service queries.
- AI answer engines when buyers ask "who can help with CRM cleanup / HubSpot / Salesforce / automation?"
- Long-tail searches from business owners, sales leaders, operations managers, and marketing teams trying to fix messy handoffs.

## Positioning Guardrails

- Keep Emergent Logic positioned as an AI-accelerated CRM consulting firm, not a generic digital agency.
- Keep claims factual: avoid "guaranteed rankings", "instant results", or inflated savings claims.
- Use practical language from real buyer pain: messy data, broken workflows, duplicate contacts, poor follow-up, unclear ownership, missing reporting, and CRM-to-website handoff gaps.
- Canada remains the local credibility base. US targeting should be remote-service friendly, especially for Salesforce admin support, HubSpot cleanup, and AI automation.
- Use external citations to support platform facts and best practices, not to over-explain obvious points.

## Revenue Priority Order

1. **Salesforce admin support and cleanup pages** because Salesforce work is higher contract value and often creates ongoing admin retainers.
2. **HubSpot cleanup / workflow repair pages** because the buying pain is immediate and easier to convert into audits.
3. **Website leads to CRM / lead capture systems pages** because this connects directly to business owners who understand lost inquiries.
4. **AI automation for CRM operations pages** because it differentiates Emergent Logic and supports higher-value automation projects.
5. **AEO / GEO support content** because it strengthens long-term visibility and answer-engine retrievability.

## Internal Linking Rules

- Every new page must link to one primary service page, one supporting blog, one case study or audit page, and `/contact`.
- Every new blog must include a mid-page contextual CTA, not only a final CTA.
- Use descriptive anchors:
  - "CRM cleanup service" instead of "click here".
  - "HubSpot workflow cleanup" instead of "learn more".
  - "website leads to CRM follow-up system" instead of "case study".
- Build these hubs:
  - Salesforce hub: Salesforce consulting, Salesforce admin support, Salesforce cleanup, Salesforce admin vs consultant.
  - HubSpot hub: HubSpot consulting, workflow cleanup, HubSpot Salesforce sync cleanup, HubSpot admin support.
  - Lead capture hub: lead capture websites, website leads to CRM, speed to lead, follow-up audit, website-to-CRM case study.
  - AI automation hub: AI automation consulting, AI chatbot lead qualification, AI revenue operations engine, AEO/GEO content.

## AEO / GEO Structure Requirements

For answer engines, pages should make the answer easy to extract:

- Start with a direct answer in the first 80-120 words.
- Use clear H2s framed as buyer questions.
- Include a concise "When you need this" section.
- Include a "What we do / what we do not do" section for trust.
- Add FAQs that answer real buying questions.
- Include service area language naturally: Surrey, Greater Vancouver, Canada, and remote support across the US where relevant.
- Cite official documentation when explaining platform behavior.
- Add Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, and Article schema where appropriate.
