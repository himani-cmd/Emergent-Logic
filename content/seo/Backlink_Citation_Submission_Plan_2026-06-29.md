## Guardrails

Emergent Logic should build authority slowly and cleanly. The goal is not to blast links. The goal is to make Google, AI search systems, local buyers, and B2B buyers see the same consistent entity everywhere: a Surrey, BC-based AI-accelerated CRM consulting firm serving Greater Vancouver, Canada, and remote clients.

Rules:

- Use the same business name everywhere: Emergent Logic.
- Use the primary URL: https://www.emergent-logic.ca/
- Use the contact page where forms or service descriptions need a conversion URL: https://www.emergent-logic.ca/contact
- Use Surrey, BC as the primary location, with remote service across Canada.
- Avoid exact-match spam anchors such as "best HubSpot consultant Vancouver" in directory profiles.
- Avoid link farms, paid guest-post networks, PBNs, scraped directories, and fake review platforms.
- Do not submit duplicate profiles under different names.
- Do not claim certifications, partner status, or client results unless verified.
