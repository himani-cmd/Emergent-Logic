## Brand Rules

- Keep the hex-node mark visible.
- Use short headlines with one idea only.
- Avoid stuffing a full post into the graphic.
- Graphic headline should create curiosity; caption should explain.
- Keep body copy off the image unless it is a checklist or workflow.

## Recommended Sizes

- LinkedIn feed: use square `1080 x 1080` or portrait `1080 x 1350`.
- Instagram feed: use portrait `1080 x 1350` when possible.
- Carousel cover: use square `1080 x 1080`.
- Story/reel cover: adapt the portrait template to `1080 x 1920` later.

## How To Use

1. Open the SVG in Canva, Figma, Illustrator, or a browser.
2. Replace the text marked by the `EDITABLE TEXT` comments.
3. Export as PNG.
4. Use the same caption across LinkedIn and Instagram with minor edits.
