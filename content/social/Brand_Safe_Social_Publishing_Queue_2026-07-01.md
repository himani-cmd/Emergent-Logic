## Brand Rule

Do not generate freeform new visual styles.

Use the approved visual system and assets:

- `content/social/assets/approved/emergent_logic_facebook_cover.png`
- `content/social/assets/approved/emergent_logic_facebook_page_profile.png`
- `content/social/assets/approved/emergent_logic_instagram_profile.png`
- `content/social/assets/approved/emergent_logic_social_icon.png`

Visual style must stay:

- white/light background,
- navy headline,
- indigo accent,
- calm layout,
- clean structure,
- no loud gradients,
- no meme-style visuals,
- no generic SaaS hype.

## Batch 3: HubSpot Workflow Cleanup

old enrollment rules,
duplicate automation,
unclear lifecycle triggers,

stale properties,
and handoffs that only one person understands.

Before building the next workflow, it is worth asking:

Can we explain what the current ones do?

If not, cleanup is the work.
