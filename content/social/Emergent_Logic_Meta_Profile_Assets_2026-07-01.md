## Design Notes

- The Facebook cover carries the wording, service positioning, and regional line.
- The profile images are symbol-only so they remain clear when cropped into circles.
- The visual style follows the Emergent Logic brand: white space, navy, indigo, light grid, and CRM-system/node motifs.
- Avoid adding small text to Instagram or Facebook profile images; it will disappear in feed comments and mobile search.
