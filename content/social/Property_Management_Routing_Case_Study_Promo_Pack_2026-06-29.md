## LinkedIn Founder Post

Property management companies usually do not lose owner opportunities because one form is broken.

They lose them because everything lands in the same messy place:

- owner inquiries
- rental evaluation forms
- realtor referrals
- tenant questions
- vendor messages
- phone calls
- emails

The team is busy, but the CRM does not clearly show:

- who owns the inquiry
- where it came from
- whether it is a real owner opportunity
- what stage it is in
- when the next follow-up should happen

That is where revenue leaks.

The first fix is not more automation.

The first fix is cleaner routing.

Separate owner leads from support noise.
Track inquiry type and source.
Assign ownership.
Create next follow-up dates.
Keep proposal follow-up visible.

Then automation becomes useful.
