## Positioning

AI chatbot automation should be positioned as a CRM-connected lead qualification service, not as a generic website widget. The message: the chatbot only matters if it creates clean CRM records, assigns ownership, and triggers follow-up.

## LinkedIn Founder Post

A lot of businesses are adding AI chatbots to their websites.

That is fine.

But the chatbot is not the real system.

The real system is what happens after the conversation:

- Was a CRM contact created?
- Was the inquiry classified?
- Was the source tracked?
- Was an owner assigned?
- Was a follow-up task created?
- Did the conversation summary make it into the CRM?
- Can the team report on chatbot-sourced qualified leads?

If the answer is no, the chatbot is just another inbox.

We are now treating AI chatbots as a CRM automation service, not a standalone widget.

The goal is not “talk to our bot.”

The goal is:

- qualify visitors
- capture clean lead data
- route inquiries
- summarize context
- trigger follow-up
- keep humans in control
