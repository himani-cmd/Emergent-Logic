## Core Positioning

Emergent Logic helps service businesses clean up CRM data, route leads properly, automate follow-up, and connect website/chat/form inquiries into HubSpot, Salesforce, or another CRM system.

We are strongest when the prospect has one of these pains:

- CRM data is messy or duplicated.
- HubSpot or Salesforce exists but is underused.
- Website leads go to email instead of a tracked CRM workflow.
- Follow-up is inconsistent across agents, advisors, consultants, or sales reps.
- They are hiring for CRM admin, marketing automation, RevOps, sales ops, or lead management.
- They have multiple forms, channels, or inboxes and no clean routing process.
