# SafeBreach Public Marketing Operations Bridge Brief

Prepared: 2026-07-14
Prepared by: Emergent Logic
Source basis: Public SafeBreach temporary Senior Marketing Operations Manager role only

## Public Signal

SafeBreach is hiring a four-month temporary Senior Marketing Operations Manager to maintain global marketing operations. The public brief includes HubSpot and Salesforce administration, database health, lead review, attribution cleanup, reports, alerts, forms, chatbot routing, webinars, and investigation of AI workflows.

## Working Inference To Validate

A temporary coverage window creates two operational risks that are worth controlling early: daily work must continue without introducing unreviewed changes, and the next owner must receive enough evidence to understand what ran, what changed, and what still needs attention.

## First Ten Working Days

1. **Establish the control plane.** List owners, access boundaries, critical systems, scheduled programs, integration dependencies, and escalation contacts. Do not request passwords or copy private records into the brief.
2. **Protect recurring operations.** Confirm the calendar and acceptance checks for campaigns, webinars, list operations, forms, alerts, reports, and high-impact workflows.
3. **Test one revenue path.** Trace a small sample from inbound capture through lifecycle assignment, routing, Salesforce visibility, follow-up, attribution, and reporting.
4. **Create the handoff ledger.** Record decisions, workflow changes, observed exceptions, unresolved risks, owners, and next review dates as work happens.

## Low-Risk Deliverables

- System, process, and ownership map.
- Critical workflow and integration inventory.
- Recurring operations calendar with acceptance checks.
- Evidence from one inbound-to-opportunity path test.
- Prioritized risk backlog and end-of-coverage handoff pack.

## Decision Question

Which failure would create the most near-term pipeline risk during the coverage window: capture and routing, campaign execution, attribution, or reporting?

## Boundary

This brief uses public information only. Emergent Logic has no internal SafeBreach access and makes no claim about the condition of SafeBreach's systems. The proposed work is a discovery and continuity framework, not a diagnosis.
