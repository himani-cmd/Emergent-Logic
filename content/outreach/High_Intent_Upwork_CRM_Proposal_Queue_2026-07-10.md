## Proposal 5 - Accounting Firm HubSpot Setup

Hi,

For an accounting firm, the most important part of HubSpot setup is making every inquiry visible and making the next action unambiguous.

I would configure the project around a simple client journey: website inquiry -> consultation request -> qualification -> follow-up -> won, lost, or long-term nurture. That includes the required properties, lifecycle and deal stages, forms, assignment, task creation, email sequences, and a dashboard for response time and pipeline movement.

I recommend starting with one service line and one inquiry path, validating it end to end, then extending the pattern. That gives you a usable system quickly without creating a large, fragile build.

Himani
