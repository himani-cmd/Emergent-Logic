# BDR Named-Contact Research Sprint

Prepared: 2026-06-29  
Goal: upgrade the high-intent prospect queue from generic accounts to specific, pain-signal-driven outreach.

## Research Standard

Do not draft from the company name alone.

For each account, the BDR should capture:

- Named decision-maker if publicly available
- Title
- Public source URL
- CRM / RevOps / Sales Ops / Marketing Ops signal
- Specific observation for email opening line
- Best outreach angle
- Whether to use direct email, LinkedIn, or contact form

## Drafting Guidance

Use 90-130 words.

Do not include long raw URLs in the body unless needed. Prefer:

- "I can send over a short CRM follow-up checklist."
- "Open to a quick review of one handoff path?"
- "Would a CRM hygiene review be useful?"

Avoid:

- "I saw you use HubSpot/Salesforce" unless the source confirms it.
- Long case-study links in first touch.
- Multi-paragraph explanations.
- Generic "we help companies grow" language.
