## Qualification Lens

This batch is focused on companies that are more likely to have CRM, RevOps, lifecycle, routing, data, or automation complexity.

High-fit signals:

- B2B SaaS or technology-enabled services
- Multiple sales channels or inbound lead paths
- Sales, RevOps, Marketing Ops, Customer Success, or Partnerships teams
- Complex customer lifecycle beyond a simple contact form
- Public hiring for RevOps, CRM, Sales Ops, Salesforce, HubSpot, lifecycle marketing, or marketing operations
- Multiple locations, advisors, agents, consultants, reps, or service lines
