## Allowed Derivatives

The masters may be uniformly resized with aspect ratio preserved. They may be
placed unchanged on an approved layout or holding field. A derivative must not
redraw, crop, recolour, isolate, retype, trace, or reinterpret any part of the
artwork.

The profile, favicon, schema, and legacy compatibility files generated by
`content/brand/generate_approved_logo_derivatives.py` are faithful size or
placement derivatives of the approved primary master.

## Deprecated Marks

- `components/ELLogo.jsx`

- previous `public/hex-logo-original.png` artwork before the reconciliation

- symbol-only reconstructions, `EL` roundels, and retyped wordmarks

Historical documents may retain earlier marks as records, but they must not be
reused, republished, or treated as templates for new work.
